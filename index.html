About Our School

Z P Boys High School, Cheepurupalli was established in 1947, the year of Indian independence. It has since grown into a beacon of education and empowerment for rural youth.

Thousands of students have walked these corridors, gaining not only academic knowledge but also moral values, leadership qualities, and life skills.

We continue to uphold our rich legacy and strive to shape the future with innovation, discipline, and a strong commitment to excellence.
