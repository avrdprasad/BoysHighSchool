About Our School

Welcome to Z P Boys High School, Cheepurupalli. Our institution is committed to providing quality education and holistic development for rural students.

Z P Boys High School, Cheepurupalli was established in the year 1947, the same year our nation gained independence. Since then, our school has stood as a pillar of knowledge and progress for the people of this region.

Thousands of students have passed through our gates, gaining not only academic knowledge but also values, discipline, and life skills. Many of our alumni have gone on to build successful careers and lives across India and around the world ‚Äî in education, engineering, medicine, business, and public service.

We are proud of our rich heritage and remain committed to shaping responsible, capable citizens who will continue to make a positive impact in society.
